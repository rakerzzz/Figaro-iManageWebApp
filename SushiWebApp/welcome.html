Our Curriculum

Students watch a video every day and learn how to process fish, etc.

Fish will be sent to students twice a week. Fish will be sent 24 times in total.

Students go to a sushi restaurant every weekend and receive a lecture from a sushi chef.

Total of 12 times.

An examination will be given after the completion of all classes.

Students who fail the exam will be required to pay an additional fee to take the exam again.

Upon successful completion, a paper certificate is sent to the student.
